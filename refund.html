Refund Policy

Fair, transparent, and hassle-free cancellations.

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Refund Eligibility

We offer refunds based on the timing of your cancellation and specific circumstances:

  • More than 2 hours before pickup: 100% refund.
  • 30 mins to 2 hours before pickup: 75% refund (25% processing fee).
  • Less than 30 minutes before pickup: 50% refund.
  • No-show or failure to be present: No refund.

2. Service Issues

If we fail to provide the service as promised, you may be eligible for a full refund:

  • Saathi partner fails to arrive for scheduled pickup.
  • Storage location is unavailable or inaccessible.
  • Significant delays beyond our estimated timeframes.
  • Technical issues preventing service completion.

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  • Cancel your booking through the Storezee app.
  • Contact our support team within 24 hours.
  • Provide your booking ID and reason for refund.
  • Submit any required documentation.

5. Processing Time

Refund processing times vary by payment method:

  • UPI/Digital wallets: 1-2 business days.
  • Credit/Debit cards: 3-7 business days.
  • Net banking: 2-5 business days.

Note: Processing times may be longer during high-volume periods or bank holidays.

6. Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may apply in situations such as service partially completed before cancellation, late cancellations, or changes to service requirements mid-booking.

7. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be provided in cases of no-show without prior cancellation, incorrect pickup information, violation of terms, or storage of prohibited items.

8. Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with our refund decision, please contact our support team. You may request escalation to management if needed.

9. Credits and Vouchers

In some cases, we may offer service credits or vouchers (valid for 12 months) instead of cash refunds for service inconveniences.
